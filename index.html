Commission Process (Paid Only)

01

Open a Ticket

Press the "Order Commissions" button in #⌈🎫⌉order-here. Then fill out the information form about your build

02

Planning & Discussing

Once a builder has responded, they will ask for some more detail about your project, Just to make sure we have everything correct.

03

Payment

Once we are done discussing and planning about your build. We will start working on your project, then the client will have to pay us 50% of the decided cost for your build

04

Building Process

During the building process the client can request images of the build. After the build is complete and the client is happy with the final product. The client will have to pay the rest of the agreed upon amount.

05

Final Delivery

After all of the previous steps have been complete. We will send the world file and .schematic file to the client.
