Commission Process (Paid Only)

01

Contact

Tell me your idea, style, requirements, and discuss payment. (Paypal, cashapp, or any other agreed payment)

02

Planning

We discuss designs, references, and details. Once we're both happy with the details the client will give me 50% of the payment for the project.

03

Building

I create your Minecraft project with updates as needed.

04

Delivery

Once the client is happy with the build, they will send me the rest of the 50% of the payment we agreed upon. Then I will send all the necessary files. (.schem, world file.)
