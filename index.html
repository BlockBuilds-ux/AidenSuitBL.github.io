How a commission runs

Four steps, start to reveal

01

Commission

You send references, dimensions and a budget tier through the form below. We reply with scope and a fixed quote — no hourly surprises.

02

Blueprint

We draft a footprint and elevation plan for your approval before any blocks go down, so changes are cheap while it's still a plan.

03

Build

Construction happens on your world or a private staging server, with progress screenshots shared at each major milestone.

04

Walkthrough

A guided in-game tour on handover, plus a wiring or storage guide for anything mechanical we built.
